Our Story

A warm little corner made for comfort, coffee, and connection.

At Dairies Café, we believe that even the smallest moments deserve something warm and comforting. What started as a simple idea — a space to pause and breathe — has grown into a place where people can find a little calm in the middle of their daily chaos.

Every cup we serve is crafted with care, and every detail in our space is designed to make you feel at home. Whether you're here to study, catch up with friends, or simply unwind, Dairies Café is your quiet corner.

“A Cup of Comfort.”

Team Responsibilities

Owner / Founder

Leads the vision and branding of Dairies Café, manages business growth, oversees finances, and ensures the café maintains its cozy identity.

Café Manager

Supervises daily café operations, organizes staff schedules, handles customer service, and keeps everything running smoothly.

Head Barista

Trains baristas, maintains drink quality standards, and creates a welcoming coffee experience for every guest.

Kitchen Lead

Oversees food preparation, kitchen cleanliness, ingredient management, and kitchen coordination.

Baristas

Prepare beverages, assist customers warmly, and maintain cleanliness around the coffee station.

Servers

Take customer orders, serve food and drinks, and ensure guests feel comfortable during their visit.

Kitchen Staff

Assist with food preparation, organize ingredients, and support smooth daily kitchen operations.
